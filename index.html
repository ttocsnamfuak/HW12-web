Summary - Latitude and Weather


In this project we are examining the effects latitude has on different

weather components. We examine the following items



Latitude verse temperature. How does the temperature vary based on the further north and south you go


Latitude verse clouds. How are clouds effected by the latitude


Latitude verse humidity. Is humidity effected by the latitude


Latitude verse wind. How is wind effected by latitude


Now it is important to also note that the weather is dependent upon a lot of variables that we are not able to account for with this simple analaysis. A more indepth analsys should be undertaken before making any specific decisions or taking any actions.
